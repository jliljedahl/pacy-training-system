TRAINING BRIEF - EFFECTIVE LEADERSHIP

Project Name: Effective Leadership for First-Time Managers

Learning Objectives:
- Understand the key responsibilities of a team leader
- Learn how to give constructive feedback
- Master basic delegation techniques
- Build trust within your team

Target Audience:
Recently promoted team leaders in tech companies with 0-2 years of management experience. They are typically aged 28-35, coming from individual contributor roles.

Desired Outcomes:
Participants should be able to:
- Conduct effective one-on-one meetings with team members
- Delegate tasks appropriately based on team member skills
- Give feedback that motivates rather than discourages
- Handle basic conflict situations

Deliverables: Articles and quizzes

Number of Chapters: 4

Language: English

Timeline: Complete within 3 weeks
Budget: Standard package

Particular Angle: Focus on practical, immediately applicable techniques. Use real-world scenarios from tech startups.
